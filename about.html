About Nat

I am a queer, trans, latinx, midwestern, multidisciplinary artist who creates pieces that range in size and function. I am drawn to found objects and recycled materials because of the deep-seated guilt of being a wasteful human, and the rush of finding a shiny piece of junk on the sidewalk. My creative process is primarily driven by intuition, emotion, and experience.

I am currently a freshman at the Cleveland Institute of Art studying Sculpture and Expanded Media. This site is a living portfolio which will be updated frequently and follow my academic journey as well as my personal studio practice. Please feel free to reach out to me via Instagram or email natlenington@gmail.com. Thank you for visiting!
